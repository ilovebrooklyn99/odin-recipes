Soy Honey Glazed Salmon Recipe

Description

This is the recipe for a soy sauce honey glazed salmon! Delicious.

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Piece of salmon
  • Minced Garlic
  • Siracha

Steps

  1. Make your salmon marinade - combine the soy sauce, honey, garlic, and siracha.
  2. Mix the salmon with the marinade and leave it in the fridge for at least half an hour.
  3. Air fry for 12 minutes at 400F.
  4. Eat with some rice and veggies and enjoy!
