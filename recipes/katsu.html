Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description

Chicken katsu! These fried chicken cutlets go great with some rice and any delicious sauce or curry.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Chicken cutlets or sliced chicken breast
  • Japanese curry

Steps

  1. Cover each chicken with flour.
  2. Then cover each chicken with the beaten egg.
  3. Finally, cover each chicken with the panko.
  4. Shallow fry or air fry, and you're done!
